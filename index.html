Content Strategy: 6 Core Pillars

1. Product Category Spotlights

Detailed posts about specific supplement categories (protein, creatine, women's supplements, etc.) with educational content about their benefits and usage recommendations tailored to Georgian fitness enthusiasts.

2. Workout & Nutrition Programs

Highlight the existing program offerings (ვარჯიში / კვება) with supplement pairing recommendations and success stories from program participants.

3. Women's Wellness Content

Dedicated content for the "WOMAN" product line addressing female-specific fitness and nutritional needs, creating an inclusive brand voice that speaks to this important audience segment.

4. Product Bundle Guides

Educational content explaining the strategic product bundles (ბანდლები), their synergistic benefits, and cost advantages compared to individual purchases.

5. Expert Video Content

Expand the YouTube channel (ჩვენი იუთუბ არხი) with expert-led content including supplement reviews, workout demonstrations, and nutritional guidance from Georgian fitness professionals.

6. Promotional and Loyalty Content

Strategic communications highlighting special offers, leveraging the newsletter signup (მეტი ფასდაკლებსთვის) to deliver personalized promotions based on customer interests and purchase history.
